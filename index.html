About Us

TrashCash is a Developers in Vogue birthed company that seeks to solve the problem of waste disposal digitally. We are driven by the saying “In someone’s trash is found another’s treasure.” Seeing the use of the large volumes of waste generated, we intend to create a safe environment by rewarding citizens for the waste they help us dispose off properly.

MISSION

Our Mission is to aid waste management through recycling, incrase income by creating wealth with the concept of trash for cash and maintain a healthy and Sustainable Environment.


VISION

Our Vision is to be the leading platform to aid in recycling accross the country and beyound. Thus increasing income and managing waste.

Our Services

TrashCash platform seeks to promote environmental sanitation and healthy lifestyle by rewarding clients for helping us manage waste. The Recyclable wastes are processed by our partners into new products for reuse. Below are our available Solutions for Recycling:

Plastic Waste

Plastic waste is any waste made of synthetic and semi-synthetic materials such us water sachets, plastic bottles, polythene bags among others that have been rejected.

Paper Waste

Paper waste is any waste inform of cards, newspapers, papers and cartons that have been rejected.

Metals

Metal waste can consists of any equipment recovered from bulky machine parts and other metal related items.
